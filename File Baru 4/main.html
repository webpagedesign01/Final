Lake Toba

Komodo Island

Komodo, island of the Lesser Sunda Islands, Nusa Tenggara Timur provinsi (province), Indonesia.

Raja Ampat

Raja Ampat is one of the most beautiful places on earth. Most scuba divers know of the incredible underwater marine life in West Papua

Bromo Mountain

Mount Bromo is an active somma volcano and part of the Tengger mountains, in East Java, Indonesia.

Candi Borobudur

Borobudur Temple serves as a Buddhist place of worship, contemplation, and meditation. The temple's art features the life, stories, and teachings of Buddha.

Rendang

Rendang is a meat dish originating from Minangkabau, West Sumatra, Indonesia.

Tumpeng

tumpeng is an Indonesian cone-shaped rice dish served typically at the selametan ceremony and other events.

Sate

Sate is a seasoned and skewered meat dish that originated from the Indonesian island of Java.

Gado-Gado

Gado-gado is an Indonesian version of a mixed salad. It usually consists of a variety of vegetables, eggs, tempeh, and tofu.
